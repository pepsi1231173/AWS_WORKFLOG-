**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [AWS Fundamentals and FCJ Onboarding](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Unity Basics and AWS IAM](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [AWS Account Access, EC2, and VPC Networking](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [EC2 Storage, AMI, and Amazon RDS](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Auto Scaling Web Architecture and AWS Budgets](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [CloudWatch Monitoring and AWS Support](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Core AWS Services and VM Import/Export](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Docker App Deployment and IAM Policy Management](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Unity Lobby and Multiplayer Systems](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Player UI and Architecture Diagram](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Game Menu, Online UI, and Enter Room](1.11-week11/)
